<h1>Go Kart</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
Refurbishing a go-kart taught valuable lessons in problem-solving and mechanical repair. The process involved diagnosing issues, sourcing parts, and reassembling components to restore optimal function. Attention to detail was crucial in identifying worn-out or damaged parts, while troubleshooting helped develop a better understanding of how each system interacts. The experience also highlighted the importance of precision and safety when working on mechanical projects.

<br />


<h2>Languages and Utilities Used</h2>

- <b>PowerShell</b> 
- <b>Diskpart</b>

<h2>Environments Used </h2>

- <b>Windows 10</b> (21H2)
